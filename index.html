Ladder Description

Welcome to Jordan's ladder! The ladder is an online ranking system for the game of racquetball, where anyone can join by providing a username, email address, and password. Each participant has a unique rank, representing her or his overall standing in the game relative to the other players.

Having rank 1 means being at the top of the ladder. You can challenge other players who are within three positions above you on the ladder to attempt to beat one of them in a head-to-head competition, and a recipient must accept your challenge before you can compete. Only players who are not already participating in an accepted challenge are available for being challenged. The winner of the match is whoever wins the best three out of five games. If you manage to win a match against a higher-ranked player, you will move up on the ladder, increasing in rank.
