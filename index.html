Ladder Description

Welcome to Jordan's ladder! The ladder is an online ranking system for the game of racquetball, where anyone can join by providing a username, email address, and password. Each participant has a unique rank, representing her or his overall standing in the game relative to the other players.

Having rank 1 means being at the top of the ladder. You can challenge other players who are within three positions above you on the ladder to attempt to beat one of them in a head-to-head competition, and a recipient must accept your challenge before you can compete. Only players who are not already participating in an accepted challenge are available for being challenged. The winner of the match is whoever wins the best three out of five games, each of which must be won by reaching a score of 15 and leading by at least 2 points. In the case that a player scores 15 points but is not leading by 2, the first player to have 2 points more than the other will win the game.

If the lower ranked player wins the match, that person will take the higher-ranked player's position on the ladder, and the loser will move down one rank. All players who were between them will also move down one rank, although this will not interfere with any previously scheduled matches. If the higher-ranked player wins the match, the ladder will remain unchanged.
